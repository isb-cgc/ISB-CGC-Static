About Us

The ISB Cancer Gateway in the Cloud (ISB-CGC) is a platform that serves as a large-scale repository and provides the computational infrastructure necessary to carry out cancer research at unprecedented scales.

How to Cite Us

Please cite the original ISB-CGC paper when you use ISB-CGC resources:

Sheila M. Reynolds, Michael Miller, Phyliss Lee, et al.
The ISB Cancer Genomics Cloud: A Flexible Cloud-Based Platform for Cancer Genomics Research
Cancer Res 2017 Nov;77(21): e7–10. 10.1158/0008-5472.CAN-17-0617

Please also consider citing this more recent review of the CRDC cloud resources, which includes ISB-CGC:

David Pot, Zelia Worman, Alexander Baumann, et al.
NCI Cancer Research Data Commons: Cloud-based Analytical Resources
Cancer Res. 2024 May;84(9):1396–1403 10.1158/0008-5472.CAN-23-2657
